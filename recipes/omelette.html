How to Make an Omelette!

Description of Recipe

In particular, I am going to teach you how to make make an amazing loaded omelette, with quite a few tasty toppings. One piece of cookware you will need is a pan. You will also need a spatula and cooking oil.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Cheese
  • Mushrooms
  • Onions
  • Tomatoes
  • Bacon

Cooking Steps

  1. Crack the eggs into a bowl.
  2. Whisk the eggs until the whites and yellows are mixed.
  3. Add salt and pepper, then pour the mixture into the pan.
  4. Once the eggs have formed a permanent shape, add toppings and cheese.
  5. Flip the omelette over the toppings
  6. Wait until the toppings are fully cooked and the cheese is melty. Enjoy!
