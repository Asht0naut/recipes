How to Make Sushi!

Description of Recipe

In particular, I am going to teach you how to make make an amazing california roll. First you will need a method of cooking rice. Next, you will need a bamboo mat to roll the sushi on.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Vinegar
  • Seaweed
  • Avocado
  • Crab
  • salt

Cooking Steps

  1. Place the nori onto the bamboo mat.
  2. Spread the sushi rice evenly onto the Nori.
  3. Flip the nori over so that the rice is on the bottom.
  4. Lay the crab sticks along the nori.
  5. Top with avocado and cucumber.
  6. Roll the sushi with the bamboo mat and cut the rolls on a cutting board.
