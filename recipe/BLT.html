How to Make a BLT!

Description of Recipe

I am going to teach you how to make a great tasting BLT. The only equipment you need to make one is a knife, a cutting board, and a cooking pan. Make sure not to microwave your bacon or it will ruin the sandwich.

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Butter
  • Bacon
  • Lettuce
  • Tomatoes
  • Mayo

Cooking Steps

  1. Spread butter on the bread and toast it on the pan
  2. Once the bread is sufficiently toasted, cook the bacon.
  3. Chop the lettuce and tomatoes.
  4. Form the sandwich.
  5. Add as much or as little mayo as you would like. Enjoy!
